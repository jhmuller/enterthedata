\framesubtitle{\forecasting}

What: forecast the next month's change in non-farm payroll
How: regress this month change on 
\begin{itemize}
\item last 12 months of changes (monthly)
\item JOLTS (Job Offering and Layoffs} (monthly)
\item weekly unemployment claims (weekly)
\item ADP payroll changes (monthly)
\item University of Michigan consumer sentiment survey (monthly)
\item Google Trends for phrases like "unemployment", "Jobs" (hourly)
\end{itemize}
